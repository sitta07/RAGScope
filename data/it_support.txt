IT Support Guidelines
1. Password Policy: Passwords must be updated every 90 days and must contain at least one special character.
2. VPN Access: To access internal servers from home, you must use the Cisco AnyConnect VPN.
3. Software Installation: Employees are not allowed to install unauthorized software on company machines.
4. Hardware Issues: For any laptop malfunction, open a ticket at support.company.com or call ext 5555.
5. Data Security: Do not share sensitive customer data via email. Use the encrypted file transfer portal.
