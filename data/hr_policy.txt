Company HR Policy 2025
1. Working Hours: Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
2. Remote Work: Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week upon manager approval.
3. Leave Policy: All employees are entitled to 15 days of paid annual leave and 10 days of sick leave.
4. Health Insurance: The company provides full health coverage including dental and vision for all full-time employees.
5. Performance Review: Reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December.
